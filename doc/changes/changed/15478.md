- `dune describe workspace` now reports an `origin` field for every module,
  indicating whether its source was written by hand (`source`), generated by a
  `rule` stanza (`dune`, with the generating dune file), produced by `ocamllex`,
  `ocamlyacc`, or `menhir`, or synthesised by dune itself for wrapping
  (`wrapper`). Generated modules point at the responsible file. The
  `--with-origin-deps` and `--with-origin-deps-expanded` flags additionally
  report the dependencies of a `rule`-generated module, as declared and expanded
  to concrete files respectively.
  (#15478, @nat-karmios)
