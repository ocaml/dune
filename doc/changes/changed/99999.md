- `dune describe workspace` now reports an `origin` field for every module,
  indicating whether its source was written by hand (`source`), generated by a
  `rule` stanza (`dune`), produced by `ocamllex` (`lexer`) or
  `ocamlyacc`/`menhir` (`parser`), or synthesised by dune itself for wrapping
  (`wrapper`). Generated modules point at the responsible file.
